Join the Ranks of Starhawk

Are you ready to embark on a journey beyond the stars? Starhawk is always on the lookout for skilled and motivated individuals to join our ranks. As a member of our mercenary fleet, you'll find yourself at the forefront of the galaxy's most thrilling challenges.

Pilot:

Commandeer cutting-edge starships, from sleek interceptors to massive capital ships. Our pilots are the vanguard of Starhawk, navigating the cosmos with precision and skill.

Ground Operative:

For those who prefer boots on the ground, our ground operatives are the backbone of Starhawk's success. From hostile alien terrain to derelict space stations, they fearlessly tackle any mission.

Tech Specialist:

The gears of Starhawk turn smoothly thanks to our tech specialists. If you have a knack for deciphering alien codes, hacking into enemy systems, or engineering innovative solutions, your skills are in high demand.

Benefits of Joining Starhawk Apart from being part of the galaxy's most renowned mercenary fleet

Competitive Compensation:

Your skills deserve recognition, and we ensure you're duly rewarded for your expertise.

Dynamic Assignments:

Every mission is a new adventure. From routine patrols to high-stakes covert operations, Starhawk keeps you on your toes.

Camaraderie:

In the vastness of space, a tight-knit community is crucial. At Starhawk, you're not just a member; you're family.
