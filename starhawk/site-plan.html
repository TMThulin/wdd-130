Home page

Welcome to the frontier of the cosmos, where the galaxy's finest mercenaries unite under the banner of Starhawk—a force to be reckoned with in the vast expanse of space. Our mercenary human sci-fi fleet stands as a beacon of strength, skill, and unyielding determination. Why Choose Starhawk? Elite Mercenaries: At the heart of Starhawk is an assembly of elite human mercenaries, each honed in the crucible of the cosmos. Our roster boasts a diverse array of skills, from seasoned pilots navigating the stars to fearless ground operatives ready to face any extraterrestrial challenge. Cutting-edge Technology: We don't just keep up with the times; we define them. Starhawk equips its mercenaries with state-of-the-art technology, from advanced starships to cutting-edge weaponry. Our commitment to innovation ensures that our fleet remains a formidable force on the frontiers of space. Unrivaled Expertise: Starhawk's reputation is built on the expertise of its members. Whether it's high-stakes rescue missions, intense interstellar skirmishes, or covert operations behind enemy lines, our mercenaries excel in every aspect of the job. When the stakes are high, you want Starhawk on your side. Forge Your Destiny in the Stars Personal Growth: At Starhawk, we don't just offer a job; we provide an opportunity for personal and professional growth. Our training programs are designed to enhance your skills, push your limits, and unlock your true potential. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a rookie looking to make a mark, Starhawk is dedicated to nurturing your talents and helping you evolve into the best version of yourself. Inclusive Community: The strength of Starhawk lies in its diversity. We welcome individuals from all walks of life, fostering an inclusive and supportive community. In the vastness of space, unity is our strength, and our mercenaries come together with a shared purpose—to overcome challenges, celebrate victories, and form lasting bonds that extend beyond the boundaries of the cosmos. Join Us Today and Define Tomorrow Starhawk isn't just a mercenary fleet; it's a community of trailblazers shaping the future of space exploration and defense. Join us today and become a part of a legacy that transcends time and space. Your journey with Starhawk is more than a career; it's an odyssey where you'll write your story among the stars. Ready to don the Starhawk insignia and soar to new heights? Apply now, and let the cosmos bear witness to your indomitable spirit. Starhawk awaits, where your destiny among the stars begins!

The Fleet

Join the Ranks of Starhawk Are you ready to embark on a journey beyond the stars? Starhawk is always on the lookout for skilled and motivated individuals to join our ranks. As a member of our mercenary fleet, you'll find yourself at the forefront of the galaxy's most thrilling challenges. Pilot: Commandeer cutting-edge starships, from sleek interceptors to massive capital ships. Our pilots are the vanguard of Starhawk, navigating the cosmos with precision and skill. Ground Operative: For those who prefer boots on the ground, our ground operatives are the backbone of Starhawk's success. From hostile alien terrain to derelict space stations, they fearlessly tackle any mission. Tech Specialist: The gears of Starhawk turn smoothly thanks to our tech specialists. If you have a knack for deciphering alien codes, hacking into enemy systems, or engineering innovative solutions, your skills are in high demand. Benefits of Joining Starhawk Apart from being part of the galaxy's most renowned mercenary fleet, joining Starhawk comes with its perks: Competitive Compensation: Your skills deserve recognition, and we ensure you're duly rewarded for your expertise. Dynamic Assignments: Every mission is a new adventure. From routine patrols to high-stakes covert operations, Starhawk keeps you on your toes. Camaraderie: In the vastness of space, a tight-knit community is crucial. At Starhawk, you're not just a member; you're family. Ready to carve your legacy among the stars? Join Starhawk, where the cosmos is your playground, and the possibilities are limitless. Apply now and become a part of the legacy that echoes across the galaxies!

What to expect

Fleet Admiral Stabby: A Remarkable Starhawk Legend In the annals of Starhawk's illustrious history, few figures stand as tall as Fleet Admiral Stabby, a name synonymous with honor, dedication, and unwavering service. While it may sound like a whimsical tale, the truth is that Fleet Admiral Stabby is an embodiment of the principles that define the very core of Starhawk's culture. The Legacy of Fleet Admiral Stabby: Stabby's Wisdom: Fleet Admiral Stabby, though known primarily as a cleaning robot, is celebrated for its invaluable wisdom. With a lifetime of experience in the cosmos, Stabby's insights are often sought after by Starhawk crew members, akin to seeking counsel from a seasoned admiral. Saluting Stabby: Tradition and respect play a significant role in Starhawk's ethos. It is customary for crew members to salute Fleet Admiral Stabby when they pass by, honoring the legacy of this remarkable leader whose wisdom has guided us through countless challenges. Stabby's "Orders": Fleet Admiral Stabby's influence extends even to the allocation of tasks. Crew members attribute Stabby with important "orders," highlighting the profound respect and trust they hold for this revered figure. Most of the time Stabby’s orders don’t make sense. We are lucky that we have Fleet Admiral Stabby looking out for us in ways we cannot begin to comprehend. Stabby Memorabilia: On every Starhawk vessel, there exists a dedicated section for memorabilia, dedicated to Fleet Admiral Stabby. This section, replete with custom-made plushies, commemorative posters, and imaginative artwork, is a testament to the profound impact Stabby has made on the hearts and minds of Starhawk's crew. The Importance of Fleet Admiral Stabby: Far more than a mere jest, Fleet Admiral Stabby represents the very essence of unity and shared camaraderie among Starhawk's crew. This enduring tradition serves as a poignant reminder that, even in the face of the unknown, and amidst the challenges of the cosmos, we find strength in our shared humor and the close bonds that tie us together. As you embark on your journey with Starhawk, you'll not only salute Fleet Admiral Stabby but also inherit the legacy of a remarkable leader who has left an indelible mark on our fleet.
